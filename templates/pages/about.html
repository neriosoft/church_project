Our beliefs

We are a Pentecostal, Christian Church and our full name is: The Body of Christ Mission (SOLUTION GROUND).

Our Vision

The Restoration, Edification, Transformation and preparation of believers through the ministry of the Word, the manifestation of the Holy Spirit and the demonstration of power with miracles, signs and wonders following. John 4:48

Our Mission

To preach the gospel (good news) of Jesus Christ to all the nations of the earth, making disciples of all kinds of people, teaching them to observe all things that Jesus Christ has commanded. Matthew 28: 19-20

Our Goal

Connecting humanity to God to live a godly life, helping them to be fruitful, subdue and dominate the earth, delivering them to live a Holy life and to possess their possessions. Obadiah 1:17

Our Belief

We believe in Trinity, that God the father, Jesus Christ his son and the Holy Spirit are one God. Man is a spirit, he has a soul and lives in the body. Water Baptism symbolizes our identification with Jesus’ death, burial and resurrection. It is the next step Christians take after conversion and having been born again at a certain age when they are old enough to take a personal decision. Matthew 28:19 The infilling and indwelling of the Holy Spirit is vital to our Christian growth, to enable us manifest the gifts of the Holy Spirit and produce the fruits of the Holy Spirit. 1 Corinthians 12:8-10, Galatians 5:22-23

Biography

He operates a prison and hospital ministry where God uses him mightily to bring restoration, healing and deliverance to many people.
