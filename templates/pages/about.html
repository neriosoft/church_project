Who we are

“But ye are a chosen generation, a royal priesthood, an holy nation, a peculiar people; that ye should shew forth the praises of him who hath called you out of darkness into his marvellous light:” 1Peter 2:9

Our Aims & Objectives

To reenact and rekindle the revival fire of the early Apostles in Acts 4:31-33 through the manifestation of the Holy Spirit, speaking the word of God with boldness, practicing the Agape love of God, and sharing all things in common.

Our Purpose

Teaching biblical doctrines, providing places of fellowship for believers, helping the helpless; widows, physically challenged, destitutes, poor and the needy.

Our Mission

To demonstrate the practical love of Christ Jesus to everyone we encounter, to be disciples who make disciples, and unifying the Body of Christ as one.

Our Vision

Helping people and nations find their way back to God.

Rev. Felix Oje Uankhoba

Lead Pastor

Woolwich Polytechnic For Boys Hutchins Road, Thamesmead SE28 8AT

Biography

He is the founder of The Body of Christ Mission in Barcelona, Kingdom of Spain, where he obtained an honor as Catalunya best man of the year award, presented by the Catalunya state government, Nigeria High commission in Spain and the Nigerian Community in Spain for his selfless service to humanity, his passion for God and compassion for people

He operates a prison and hospital ministry where God uses him mightily to bring restoration, healing and deliverance to many people.
